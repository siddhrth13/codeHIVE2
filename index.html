Frequently asked Questions

We are proud to be based in the thriving city of Bangalore, also known as the Silicon Valley of India. Our location in this bustling tech hub provides us with access to a diverse community of learners and experts, allowing us to offer top-notch coding education to our learners.

At codeHIVE, we believe in making coding education accessible to everyone. That's why we offer a free trial period of 2 weeks for all our courses, so you can try them out and see if they're right for you. After the trial period, you can choose to continue with the course and pay according to the language and mode of learning you prefer. We offer a range of pricing options, including self-paced online courses, in-person bootcamps, and personalized coaching. With our flexible pricing and learning options, you can find the course that fits your needs and budget.

At codeHIVE, we welcome learners of all skill levels to our coding courses. Our beginner-level courses are designed to be accessible to those with no prior coding experience, and we provide comprehensive learning materials to guide you through the basics. However, if you are interested in taking intermediate or advanced courses, we do recommend that you have some foundational knowledge in the relevant programming language or technology. This will ensure that you get the most out of the course and can keep pace with the material. Our course descriptions will provide more detailed information on any prerequisites required, so you can choose the course that best fits your needs and skill level.

All our coding courses can be accessed directly from our website. Once you sign up and select the course you're interested in, you'll be able to access all the course materials, including video tutorials, code samples, and practice exercises. In addition, we make all course resources available through our website's cloud storage drive, so you can easily download and save them for offline access. Our website is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, so you can focus on your learning without any technical obstacles. Whether you're a beginner just starting out or an experienced coder looking to upgrade your skills, our courses and resources are available at your fingertips.

At codeHIVE, we believe in providing not just high-quality coding education, but also tangible career benefits to our learners. Upon completion of any of our courses, you'll receive a professional certificate that you can showcase on your resume and LinkedIn profile. In addition, we offer career guidance and support services, including job search assistance and access to our network of industry experts and employers. Our goal is to help our learners take their coding skills to the next level and land their dream jobs or internships. We're committed to your success, both inside and outside the classroom, and we look forward to helping you achieve your goals.
